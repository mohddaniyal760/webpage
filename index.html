Case Studies

In-Depth Project Analysis

A closer look at some of my most impactful engineering projects — the problem, the approach, and the results.

01

Smart Home Automation with ESP32 & Mobile App

Challenge: A client wanted full home control with minimal wiring changes and remote access.

Solution: Designed an ESP32-based system with relays, mobile app via Firebase, and voice assistant integration using IFTTT. Installed non-invasively behind existing switches.

Outcome: 100% wireless control, 30% energy saving through scheduling, and complete remote monitoring.

30%
Energy Saved
02

Residential Building Electrical Layout – Prayagraj

Challenge: A 3-floor residential building needed a complete electrical layout design compliant with IS standards.

Solution: Created detailed wiring diagrams, load calculations, panel schedules, and earthing design using AutoCAD Electrical. Coordinated with the contractor for installation.

Outcome: Zero safety violations on inspection, on-time project completion, and a fully documented electrical plan for future reference.

✅
IS Compliant
03

Industrial Appliance Refurbishment Program

Challenge: A workshop had 50+ non-functional electrical devices needing cost-effective restoration.

Solution: Performed systematic fault diagnosis, component-level repair, safety testing, and performance validation on each unit. Replaced only faulty components, minimising cost.

Outcome: 92% of devices successfully restored, saving the client over 60% versus new replacement cost.

Cost Saved
